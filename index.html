Interview System — AV Sales Rep

Built for hiring meeting planners transitioning into AV sales. Two STAR frameworks, behavioral questions, and a scoring scorecard.

Why meeting planners make ideal TRS sales reps — your opening pitch to the candidate
What they already have
✓ They know the client — event planners, associations, corporates
✓ They understand AV needs firsthand
✓ They have credibility: they've been on the buying side
✓ They have an existing network of event contacts
What changes for the better
✓ No more traveling city to city
✓ Unlimited commission — not a capped salary
✓ Selling to the same clients they already know
✓ Phone/virtual-first — not constant mingling
"You already know what these clients need — I'm just putting you on the other side of the table with a commission structure."
Green flags — hire signals
✓ Specific numbers in their answers
✓ Talks about following up consistently
✓ Comfortable saying "I don't know — I'd figure it out"
✓ Has chased down a client or deal before
✓ Shows curiosity about how AV works
✓ Asks about commission structure early
Red flags — pass signals
✗ Vague answers with no metrics or specifics
✗ Blames others when deals fell through
✗ Says "I'm not really a phone person"
✗ Can't describe a time they persuaded someone
✗ Only comfortable with warm/inbound leads
✗ Avoids talking about rejection or failure
How to use STAR questions
S

T

A

What number proves it?
If they can't get to a number or specific outcome on the Result, probe: "What did that lead to?" / "How did you measure success?" / "How much revenue / time / clients did that affect?"

⚡ Odoo Quick Reference

The 10 most common actions — open this on a second screen during live calls in Week 2.

📋
Log a call note on a lead
1

Open CRM → find your lead → click it

2

Scroll to Chatter at the bottom

3

Click Log Note (not Send Message)

4

Write: date · who you spoke to · what they said · next step

5

Click Add

Log Note = internal only. Send Message = goes to client. Never mix them up.
⏰
Schedule a follow-up activity
1

Open the lead in CRM

2

Click the clock icon (Activities button) near the top

3

Type: Phone Call · set due date · add a note

4

Click Schedule

Never leave a lead without a scheduled next activity. No next step = dead lead.
📊
Move a lead to a new stage
1

CRM → switch to Kanban view (grid icon, top right)

2

Click and drag the lead card to the correct column

3

Or open the lead → click the stage name in the top status bar

Stages: New → Qualified → Proposition → Won. Mark Lost if dead.
❌
Mark a lead as Lost
1

Open the lead in CRM

2

Click Mark as Lost at the top of the record

3

Select a reason: Price / In-house AV / Event Cancelled / No response

4

Add a brief note → click Mark as Lost

Never delete a lead. Always mark Lost with a reason — that data matters.
📄
Create a rental quotation
1

Open Rental app → click New

2

Search and select the customer

3

Click Add a product → add rental items and quantities

4

Check the total → click Send by Email when ready

Always send from Odoo — not Gmail. It tracks opens and lets clients accept online.
📧
Send a quote to a client
1

Open the quotation in Rental

2

Click Send by Email

3

Review the email preview — confirm "To" address is correct

4

Click Send → status updates to "Quotation Sent"

After sending, log a note and schedule a follow-up activity for 2 days out.
🕐
Clock in / Clock out
1

Home dashboard → open Attendances app

2

Click Check In at start of day

3

Click Check Out at end of day

Do this every single day. Forgetting = untracked hours. First and last thing you do.
🔍
Filter to see only your leads
1

CRM → click the search bar at the top

2

Click the dropdown → Filters → My Pipeline

3

To save permanently: click ☆ → Save current search → check "Use by default"

New reps get overwhelmed seeing 200 leads. Always filter to My Pipeline first.
📅
View your activity list for today
1

CRM → My Pipeline filter applied

2

Click the Activity view icon (clock icon, top right)

3

Red = overdue (call first) · Orange = due today · Green = upcoming

Start every morning here. Never leave with red activities still open.
💬
Post your end-of-day log note
1

Employees app → open your own profile

2

Scroll to Chatter → click Log Note

3

Write: "Day X complete. [X] calls, [X] leads in pipeline. Ready for tomorrow."

4

Type @ → select your manager → click Add
